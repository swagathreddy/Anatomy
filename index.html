A classroom game to learn about ocean topography.

Instructions

1. An illustration similar to this is drawn in the room or on the playground. Look at the illustration to see the main diagram with all the zones marked.

2. Each of you will receive a feature card.

3. When the timer starts (5 minutes), study the words and the zones on the diagram carefully so you can remember them.

4. After the timer ends, read the clue on your card. Try to guess which zone or landform you belong to.

5. Walk to the correct spot in the room or playground that matches your clue.

6. Once everyone is in place, check your answers and act out the different landforms together!
