Ocean Floor Adventure 🌊

A classroom game to learn about ocean topography.

Instructions

1. Your teacher would have marked out the seafloor in your classroom or playground.

2. When the teacher starts the timer, quickly memorize the different features of the ocean and try and match your action card to the feature of the ocean.

3. When the image disappears from the screen, the teacher signals you to run to the landform to which you belong.

4. Remember to hold the position as shown in the card. Once you have taken your positions, the teacher will reveal the answers
